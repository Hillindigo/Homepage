Hillindigo is a personal digital garden in progress. It will hold polished essays, unfinished notes, project retrospectives and observations from life beyond technology.

## Why this place exists

Information moves quickly, while durable understanding forms slowly. This site gives technical practice and personal thought a stable, searchable and maintainable home.

## What will live here

- AI agents and software development workflows
- Frontend, backend and engineering practice
- Knowledge management and personal tools
- Industrial AI research
- Reading, photography and fragments of life

## A note on writing

Articles here will continue to evolve. The publication date marks the first public version, while the updated date records meaningful revisions.
