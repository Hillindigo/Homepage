Today the multi-page structure of Hillindigo became real. Short notes will stay here—close to the moment an idea appears. Some may eventually grow into essays; others can remain small and useful.

## A working rule

Capture first. Revisit with evidence. Promote only when the idea deserves a more complete argument.
