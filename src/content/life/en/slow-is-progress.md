After too much time in front of a screen, the scarce resource is often not time but uninterrupted attention. A walk does not need a destination; it creates a span in which the mind is not required to respond immediately.

## Time without input

No podcast, no messages, no attempt to make every minute productive. When input pauses, interrupted thoughts can find each other again.

## Keep some space empty

A digital garden needs content. A life does not need to be documented at every moment. What is worth writing can arrive later.
